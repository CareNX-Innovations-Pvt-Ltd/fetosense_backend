(inner) getData(req, res, next) → {void}

Fetches mother data based on filters and enriches it with organization and device details. - Filters mothers based on date range and organization ID. - Retrieves associated organization and device information. - Adjusts timestamps to IST (UTC+5:30). - Merges organization and device details into the mother data.
